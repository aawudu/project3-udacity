Investigation Overview¶

The objective of the project is to use Python visualization libraries to explore a dataset thoroughly. The analysis begins with exploring univariate variables followed by bivariate and multivariate analysis. Variables are divided into two categories:

  1. The demorgraphic information (The basic borrower information)
  2. The loan performance indicators (Loan performance variables)

Dataset Overview¶

Data is from Prosper Loan database. The dataset has are 113,937 loans entries with 81 variables. The variables are numeric and categorical in nature where 61 variables are of numeric type and the remaing of string or object type.

Insights¶

  • Loans taken from Prosper Loan are mostly for the purposes of Debt Consolidation.
  • Default loans are mainly by borrowers whose employment status are not employed and retired with income range of $0 and $1-25k. The highest-rated loan score is 4 with 12595, which has a high risk according to the prosper scare rating. The best rating of 11 has a frequency of 1456, which is the second least on the bar chart.

We can conclude loans are normally of very high risk, even though the default rates seemed comparatively low considering completed, current and cancelled loans. Prosper loans seems to have kept in measures to reduce default rates, but more needs to be to improve risks.
